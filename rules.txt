Fuzzy Stock Market Prediction

INPUTS
-------
1. RSI (Relative Strength Index) [0-10]
    HIGH
    MIDDLE
    LOW

2. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) [0 - 10]
    LOW
    HIGH

OUTPUT
-------
1. STOCK ACTION [0 - 10]
    BUY
    HOLD
    SELL

RULES
------
1. If RSI is HIGH THEN SELL

2. If RSI is LOW OR MIDDLE, and MACD is LOW THEN HOLD

3. If RSI is LOW OR MIDDLE and MACD is HIGH THEN BUY
