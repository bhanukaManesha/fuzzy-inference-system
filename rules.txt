Fuzzy Stock Market Prediction

INPUTS
-------
1. RSI (Relative Strength Index) [0-10]
    HIGH
    MIDDLE
    LOW

2. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) [0 - 10]
    LOW
    HIGH

OUTPUT
-------
1. STOCK MARKET MOMENTUM [0 - 10]
    BEARISH
    NEUTRAL
    BULLISH

RULES
------
1. If RSI is HIGH THEN BULLISH

2. If RSI is LOW OR MIDDLE, and MACD is LOW THEN NEUTRAL

3. If RSI is LOW OR MIDDLE and MACD is HIGH THEN BEARISH
